fn add_edge(&mut self, src: AudioNodeIdx, src_ch: usize, dest: AudioNodeIdx, dest_ch: usize) -> Result<()>

Connects two devices in the graph.

  • src and dest are identifiers for the actual devices to connect.
  • src_ch and dest_ch are the channel indices of the two devices.

If invalid indices are provided, or if the specified edge would create a cycle in the graph, an Err is returned and no changes dest the graph are made.
